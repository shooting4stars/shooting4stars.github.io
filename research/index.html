Interactive concept · Equiaxed growth

Watch grains find their neighbours.

Grains nucleate in the melt and grow while rejecting solute into the surrounding liquid. As their solutal fields overlap, each grain's growth is increasingly influenced by its growing neighbours. This conceptual view illustrates that interaction without presenting numerical output from the research code.
